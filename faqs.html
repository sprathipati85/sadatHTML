Frequently Asked Questions



1. HOW CAN I BECOME A MEMBER?

Visit one of our branches, fill out the money transfer form and provide the necessary documentations.

2. WHICH COUNTRIES/TOWNS/CITIES CAN I SEND MONEY TO?

Send Money in local currency or foreign currencies to over 160 countries through our partners network, which is currently made up of 200.000 locations worldwide. Please see the list of collection points for the relevant country.

3. WHAT REQUIREMENTS ARE NEEDED TO MAKE A TRANSACTION?

You need to register with us. We also require 2 forms of identification, 1 photographic and 1 recent utility bill for amounts up to 5000.

For amounts over 5000 we require the above two identification documents as well as proof of source of your income, which can be pay slips or bank statements.

4. WHAT FORMS OF PAYMENT ARE ACCEPTED?

We accept:

4.1: Cash. Payment can be made into one of our branches in person or once you registered with us you can deposit the said monies into our nominated bank account.

4.2: Cheques are also accepted, (the transaction will only take place after the funds have cleared).

4.3: Credit or Debit card payment: At present we don't accept credit or debit card payment.

4.4: Bank to bank transfer payments are also accepted from our members. Low fees, higher security and better guarantees. You just need to indicate the specified information for the bank account to which you want to transfer the funds and we guarantee that the money will be available on deposit. Funds will arrive quickly, effectively and with guaranteed security.

5. HOW TO MAKE A BANK TRANSFER?

Wire transfers direct to bank accounts. Low fees and higher security. You just need to indicate the specified information for the bank account to which you want to transfer the funds and we guarantee that the money will be available on deposit. Funds will arrive quickly, effectively and with guaranteed security.

6. WHAT IS THE LIMIT TO TRANSFER MONEY?

The transfer limit depends on the destination country and its regulations. Please contact us for further information.

7. HOW SOON WILL THE BENEFICIARY RECEIVE THEIR MONEY?

It depends on the service you choose. Cash pick up, physical delivery (only available to selected countries) or cash Transfer into a bank Account. Cash picks are normally available on the same day, while money transferred to a bank account may take up to 3 working days. Physical cash delivery which is only available to some countries will take up to 5 working days.

8. WHERE CAN THE BENEFICIARY RECEIVE THEIR FUNDS?

Through the nominated collection point by the remitter. In some countries the centre which processes the payment will call your beneficiary to inform them of the collection point.

9. WHAT DOCUMENTATION SHOULD THE BENEFICIARY USE TO RECEIVE THE MONEY?

The beneficiary requires their country of residence national ID card. (Original and copy)

10.WILL MY PERSONAL INFORMATION BE PROTECTED?

We guarantee 100% customer confidentiality.

11. IS MY MONEY SAFE?

YES! Your money is safe and secure and will be delivered with 100% assurance to the said destination.

12. HOW WILL I KNOW THE STATUS OF MY TRANSACTION?

You can phone us to ask for an update of your transaction.

13. WHAT ARE THE CHARGES FOR THE SERVICES?

It depends on the country you sending the money to. Sending money to some countries may not require any fee, while some may have a fixed fee for any amount sent. Please contact us to find out about the fees to your respective country.

14.WILL I RECEIVE SOME SORT OF RECEIPT FOR MY TRANSACTION?

Yes you will have receive a receipt which will include your transaction unique code as well as the amount sent, exchange rate, receiving amount in local currency and if any fee charged. Receipt will also include the payout agent details along with their telephone numbers and address where your beneficiary should go and collect the money.

15. WHAT IS THE BEST TIME TO EXECUTE A TRANSACTION?

The best time for the transaction is before 5.00 pm BST.

16. WHAT IF MY TRANSACTION IS REFUSED?

Money laundering and other unlawful activities; Sadat Exchange Ltd reserves the right at all times to refuse to process any currency orders or International Money Transfers at any stage, which it believes in its sole discretion, is connected in any manner to a drug money laundering, terrorist activity or other unlawful purpose. We have a legal obligation to report to Customs and Excise any such suspicious transactions. The definition of a suspicious transaction is one that raises "an unconfirmed belief" or "a fleeting inkling".

17. I STILL HAVE ADDITIONAL QUESTIONS ABOUT THE SERVICE, WHOM SHOULD I CONTACT?

Please contact us using the details on our CONTACT US page to have your queries answered.
