About Us

Sadat Exchange has swiftly established itself as the foremost remittance company specialising in foreign exchange money transfers.

Through the continuous exchange of information, new concepts and ideas dedicated to the business of transmitting funds, Sadat Exchange developing further products whilst maintaining high levels of customer service. It is our sincere belief that understanding our customer's requirements is crucial. As such, we have amassed a wealth of market knowledge to ensure all transactions are processed speedily and efficiently.

Over the years we have established strong commercial ties with key premier paying agents. Through continuous collaboration with these agents and constant feedback, we have thus been able to create and maintain a seamless, customer friendly remittance system.

Utilising our extensive network of payees in conjunction with our local market expertise, we continue to offer our customers a safe, secure and speedy service. This ideology is reflected in our ability to offer our customers the most competitive exchange rates.

We strongly believe, by offering exceptional customer service and competitive pricing, we are in a position to maintain and enhance our market share.
